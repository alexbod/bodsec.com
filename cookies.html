Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how BodSec ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website bodsec.com.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide analytics, and remember your preferences.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary Essential for the website to function properly (e.g., security, navigation) Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our site to improve performance Up to 2 years
Functional Remember your preferences and settings for a better experience Up to 1 year

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers) that set their own cookies. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they use this information. We do not control these cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking or deleting cookies may impact your experience on our website, and some features may not function as intended.

5. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" setting. We currently do not respond to Do Not Track signals, but we respect your privacy choices and provide the cookie management options described above.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us.
